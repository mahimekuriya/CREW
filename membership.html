Regular [Full] Members.

Full members are women of Ethiopian background and/or origin. Full members have the right to be elected officers of the organization and they also are eligible to vote.

Associate Members.

Associate members can be Ethiopians or non-Ethiopians, women or men. Associate member cannot be elected officers of the organization and they are not eligible to vote.
