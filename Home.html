A Girl should be two things: who and what she wants

"Are you passionate to work on Ethiopian women's right? Center for the Rights of Ethiopian Women (CREW) is seeking motivated interns and volunteers who are interested to work in advancing the rights of Ethiopian women worldwide. CREW's interns and volunteers will get involved in many aspects of CREW's day to day work and support CREW's programs and staff while contributing to make a difference in the lives of Ethiopian Women. Please contact us at ethiowomen@gmail.com if you are interested in internship and volunteering positions with CREW.
